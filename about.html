About

Logan
Smith

Combat / Technical Designer

I'm a combat and technical designer focused on making games that feel great to play — specifically in how enemies challenge the player and how the player character responds to input.

My work centers on Unreal Engine 5, where I design and implement combat systems, enemy AI, player character movement, and the feedback systems that connect them all. I care deeply about the gap between what a designer intends and what a player actually experiences.

I've led small teams, collaborated on large ones (23 people on Unsanctified), and worked across the spectrum from solo gameplay systems to coordinating across art, engineering, and design disciplines.

Influences on my work include Ghost of Tsushima, ULTRAKILL, Curse of the Dead Gods, and the Soulsborne series — games where combat is a conversation between the player and the system, with clear rules that reward mastery.
