Logan Smith

Combat and Technical Designer

I design combat systems, enemy AI, and player character mechanics in Unreal Engine 5. My focus is on the gap between what a designer intends and what a player actually experiences - making sure those two things are as close together as possible.

I have worked across team sizes from 3 to 23 people, leading smaller teams while contributing to larger ones. Regardless of scale, the work is the same: make combat feel fair, readable, and worth mastering.

My design influences include Ghost of Tsushima, ULTRAKILL, Curse of the Dead Gods, and the Soulsborne series - games where combat is a system with clear rules that rewards learning them.
