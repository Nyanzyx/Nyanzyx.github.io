Ghost-Inspired AI & Stealth System

Inspired by Ghost of Yotei and Ghost of Tsushima, the goal of this AI system is to create enemies that are challenging, predictable, and fair — giving players readable openings to master their environment.

Under the hood, this is achieved by blending the performance of a custom C++ State Machine with the flexibility of Unreal Engine 5's AI Perception and Behavior Trees.

Core Perception Mechanics

Vision Zones — Forgiving Margins

Suspicion doesn't fill at a flat rate. Dead-center in an enemy's cone: spotted 2× faster. Periphery: fills at half speed. Gives players a crucial split-second to realize their mistake and break LOS.

Vertical Blind Spots

A custom height-limiter prevents enemies from unrealistically spotting players directly above or below them — enabling rooftop assassinations and vertical gameplay without AI cheating.

Responsive Hiding

The system checks against what actor the AI currently perceives rather than when perception updates. If a player drops into cover while being watched, the AI instantly breaks visual lock and investigates the last known location.
