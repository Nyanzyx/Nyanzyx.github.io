Combat Design - Weapon Systems - Animation - UE5

Unsanctified

A third-person soulslike action-adventure. Worked within a 23-person team to design and implement core combat systems, the weapon pipeline, player movement, and animation.

PlatformPC
EngineUnreal Engine 5
Duration13 Months
Team Size23
Playtime2 Hours
RoleCombat Designer
Detailed system writeups are being expanded for this project.

Responsibilities

  • Utilized Animation Notifies to set up animation-based attacks for the player
  • Designed and implemented the weapon system and all weapons
  • Designed and implemented all player character movement and animations
  • Iterated on the 3 C's for the player character
  • Refactored the codebase for a more streamlined team environment
  • Collaborated with artists on timing and feel of combat and locomotion animations
  • Designed enemies that fit properly within the combat sandbox

Scale and Scope

Unsanctified was the largest team project in this portfolio at 23 people over 13 months. Beyond feature work, a significant part of the role was refactoring the codebase to be navigable at that team size. Clean architecture became as important as shipping new systems.

Collaboration with the art team on animation timing and combat feel was a continuous process. Getting hit windows, attack cancels, and locomotion blending to feel correct required close back-and-forth iteration across disciplines.
